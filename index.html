Welcome

Mideast is the first Shisha Lounge in Bogor. Established in 2015, Mideast is now located in the center of Bogor that is on Jl. Sancang No.8, Babakan, where the relocation happened on 2020. To support the atmosphere of the Shisha Lounge itself, Mideast has a bold and authentic Middle Eastern concept. However, the menu items here are not only menus from the Middle Eastern, there are also several other Asian and Western menus that are no less indulgent.

The atmosphere

With a very bold middle eastern concept, Mideast is suitable for those of you who want to enjoy an atmosphere like this with your family. Because Mideast supports the atmosphere and offerings to be enjoyed with family. Apart from being with your family, Mideast can also be a place to spend your time with friends and colleagues.

Best Menu Selection

Mideast itself has several mainstay menus, one of which is Arais as an appetizer with a choice of chicken, goat, and cheese filling. This appetizing appetizer can then be followed by Kebuli Rice with Young Goat as the main course which is a recommended menu at Mideast.

Best Shisha Selection

Besides enjoying the atmosphere and menu here, don't forget to enjoy the best quality Shisha at Mideast. The various Shisha here are highly recommended and need to be tried, packages are also available such as the Horus and Osiris packages which are more affordable but do not reduce the quality and experience of your best Shisha.
