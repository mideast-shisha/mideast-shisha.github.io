Mideast Shisha Lounge

Mideast is the first Shisha Lounge in Bogor. Established in 2015, Mideast is now located in the center of Bogor that is on Jl. Sancang No.8, Babakan, where the relocation happened on 2020. To support the atmosphere of the Shisha Lounge itself, Mideast has a bold and authentic Middle Eastern concept. However, the menu items here are not only menus from the Middle Eastern, there are also several other Asian and Western menus that are no less indulgent.
