Welcome to
Mideast Shisha Lounge
Mideast is the first Shisha Lounge in Bogor. Established in 2015, Mideast is now located in the center of Bogor that is on Jl. Sancang No.8, Babakan, where the relocation happened on 2020. To support the atmosphere of the Shisha Lounge itself, Mideast has a bold and authentic Middle Eastern concept. Mideast itself has several mainstay menus, one of which is Arais as an appetizer with a choice of chicken, goat, and cheese filling. This appetizing appetizer can then be followed by Kebuli Rice with Young Goat as the main course which is a recommended menu at Mideast. Besides enjoying the atmosphere and menu here, don't forget to enjoy the best quality Shisha at Mideast. With a very bold middle eastern concept, Mideast is suitable for those of you who want to enjoy an atmosphere like this with your family. Apart from being with your family, Mideast can also be a place to spend your time with friends and colleagues. The combination of the atmosphere with the menu at Mideast will guarantee your best experience in enjoying the Resto & Shisha Lounge, especially in the city of Bogor.
